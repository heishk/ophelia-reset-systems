The 7-Day Reset Home System

A room-by-room printable declutter plan for a calmer, cleaner home.

Quick Start

  • Pick one visible surface and clear it first.
  • Create three piles: keep, move, remove.
  • Take one before photo so progress is visible.
  • Do not buy organizers until Day 7.

7-Day Plan

Day 1: Visible surfaces — counters, tables, desk, dresser tops.
Day 2: Kitchen reset — expired food, pantry zones, clear prep space.
Day 3: Fridge reset — dates, categories, leftovers, drinks, produce.
Day 4: Bedroom reset — bed, floor clothes, donation bag, bedside table.
Day 5: Bathroom reset — expired products, daily-use zone, backups away.
Day 6: Paper reset — action, archive, trash.
Day 7: Maintenance — weekly reset time, donation box, simple storage list.

Declutter Decision Tree

If you answer “no” twice, remove it, donate it, recycle it, or store it somewhere clearly labeled.

  • Have I used this in the last 90 days?
  • Would I buy it again today?
  • Does it have a clear home?
  • Is it worth cleaning, storing, and moving?
